# Game Ideas for GameHUB

Here are some games that could be added to the app. All of these are feasible to build from scratch (no iframes or external embeds required):

## Classic & Puzzle Games
- **2048**
  - Sliding tile puzzle game where you combine numbers to reach 2048.
- **Tetris**
  - Classic falling block puzzle game.
- **Minesweeper**
  - Logic puzzle game with hidden mines.
- **Sudoku**
  - Number placement puzzle (can be classic or variants).
- **Snake**
  - Guide a growing snake to eat food and avoid walls/self.
- **Connect Four**
  - Two-player disc-dropping strategy game.
- **Memory Match (Concentration)**
  - Flip cards to find matching pairs.
- **Lights Out**
  - Turn off all the lights by toggling tiles.

## Arcade & Action
- **Breakout / Idle Breakout**
  - Destroy bricks with a bouncing ball (optionally with idle/progression mechanics).
- **Asteroids**
  - Classic space shooter with drifting asteroids.
- **Pong**
  - Simple two-player paddle game.
- **Flappy Bird**
  - Tap to keep a bird flying through pipes.
- **Space Invaders**
  - Defend against waves of descending aliens.

## Strategy & Simulation
- **Tower Defense**
  - Place towers to stop waves of enemies.
- **Idle Clicker**
  - Simple incremental game (e.g., Cookie Clicker style).
- **Minesweeper Battle**
  - Competitive or co-op variant of Minesweeper.

## Word & Trivia
- **Wordle Clone**
  - Guess the hidden word in 6 tries.
- **Hangman**
  - Classic word guessing game.
- **Quiz/Trivia**
  - Multiple choice or open-ended questions.

## Multiplayer/Party
- **Tic-Tac-Toe (with online play)**
  - Classic 3x3 grid, optionally with matchmaking.
- **Battleship**
  - Guess the location of opponent's ships.

---

**Note:** All of these can be built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and can be integrated into the GameHUB app without iframes or external dependencies.
